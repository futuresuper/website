The best decisions aren’t always the hard ones.

An electric bike in winter. An eco-friendly diet. Shopping local and dressing ethically. Let’s face it - being a good human can be pretty hard work sometimes.

But the decision of where your super gets invested is an easy change that can protect your future without requiring daily sacrifices from you. This one, small decision can have an exponential impact and becomes a lifetime of good ethical and financial choices.

Here’s how investing with Future Super can compare with other actions you might take.

We’re not solving climate change. We’re investing in the companies who will.

Investing your super in things like renewable energy instead of fossil fuels isn’t just an ethical choice. It’s boarding the future at the first stop.

Big businesses make a lot of money destroying our planet and most investors just want a slice of those profits, so they let that status quo stand.

Climate change is a problem driven by companies who make money while destroying our planet. Most investors will look the other way, but we know business needs to change quickly to safeguard our planet and investments. That’s why we invest in those doing the work to prepare for a future without fossil fuels. We take money out of the hands of unsustainable companies and put it to work on investments that can thrive as the world moves to reduce emissions.

We know that just a fraction of the $3 trillion in super funds could finance Australia’s transition to 100% renewable energy. Right now, more money than that is already invested in fossil fuels by the super industry. Future Super makes it easy for you to choose clean energy instead.

Future Super invests in solar, wind, infrastructure, social housing and more. So when the world pays the price of climate recovery - it will be into your pocket.
