We invest in the companies who will.

Just 7.7% of superannuation could fund Australia’s transition to 100% renewable energy1. Right now, more money than that is already invested in fossil fuels by the super industry.

We make it easy for you to be part of the solution by investing in solar, wind, infrastructure and more. Not all your super will invest in direct climate solutions - but your money will be in low carbon portfolios made up of companies genuinely committed to a sustainable future.

Investing your super in a climate conscious fund like Future Super isn’t just an ethical choice. It’s boarding the future at the first stop. So when the world pays the price of climate recovery - it will be into your pocket.
