The super industry’s purpose is to invest and grow Australia’s retirement savings. But super funds have consistently invested money in fossil fuels and other harmful industries that have set us on a path towards climate change.

Future Super offers a better way to invest super for your future.

Our mission

We grow retirement savings by investing in a future free from climate change and inequality.

What that means

Your money will invest for your financial benefit and for the quality of your future as you save for retirement.

We seek impactful investments creating a better future.

We exclude investments with harmful impacts on people and the planet.

Investing for impact

Impact investing is what makes Future Super unique. These are investments we seek out that provide financial value to our members and also make a direct contribution to action on climate change or inequality.

Many of these involve the creation of new clean energy, while others create the infrastructure for a more equal future, like the building of disability housing.

These investments create tangible systems change that may not happen without our investment.

We seek impact investments

Choosing impact investments isn’t a cut-and-dry process, because each opportunity is unique. We seek investments that have a significant climate or social benefit, whatever it may be, in addition to financial performance now and in the future. Although that can be anything, there are some categories we keep a close eye on.

  • Clean energy
  • Speciality housing
  • Recycling and waste
  • Social impact
  • Healthcare
  • Sustainable agriculture
  • Preservation of biodiversity

We exclude harmful investments

We have industry-leading ethical screens to help exclude harm from your super. Investments must pass every screen to be included. This means they don’t work against our mission.
